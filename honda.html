Repsol Honda

Who is the Owner of this team ?

    The team is run by Honda Racing Corporation (HRC) — Honda’s official racing arm. The sponsorship and branding historically came from Repsol, a Spanish energy company, from 1995 to 2024.

Origin of the team

    The legendary Repsol Honda Team formed through a strategic collaboration between Honda and Repsol starting in 1995, blending Japanese engineering with Spanish sponsorship to form one of MotoGP’s most successful partnerships

Achievemet of the team

    Over three decades, the team clinched 15 premier-class riders’ championships, 10 constructors/team titles, an impressive 183 race wins, and over 450 podiums — making it the most successful squad in MotoGP history.

Formed in which year

    The Repsol Honda collaboration officially began in 1995, marking the inception of this powerhouse MotoGP team.

Years with major wins

    The championship victories in years like 1995–1999, 2002–2003, 2006, 2011, and 2013–2019.
