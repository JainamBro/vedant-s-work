LCR Honda

Who is the Owner of this team ?

    The team is owned and was founded by former Grand Prix rider Lucio Cecchinello. He established the team in 1996 and continues to be its driving force.

Origin of the team

     The team was created in 1996 by Lucio Cecchinello to compete in the 125cc World Championship. Over the years, it expanded, adding a 250cc effort and eventually moving into the premier MotoGP class in 2006. The team has had a long-standing partnership with Honda, although it has used other manufacturers in the past in the lower classes.

Achievemet of the team

     LCR Honda has a strong record for a satellite team, with notable wins and podiums in all classes it has competed in. Its most significant achievements include numerous race wins and podium finishes in the MotoGP class. While they have not won a Riders' or Teams' World Championship in the premier class, they have consistently been a competitive force, often punching above their weight.
