Pramac Racing

Who is the Owner of this team ?

    The team is owned by Paolo Campinoti, who is also the CEO of the Pramac group, a company that specializes in the production of power generators and material handling equipment

Origin of the team

     Pramac made its debut in MotoGP in 2002. It later forged a long-standing and highly successful partnership with Ducati in 2005, becoming their primary satellite team

Achievemet of the team

    While a satellite team, Pramac has achieved remarkable success. In 2023, they won the MotoGP Teams' World Championship, a historic achievement as the first independent team to do so. They have also secured numerous race wins, podium finishes, and pole positions throughout their history with Ducati

Formed in which year

     The team was founded in 2002, but its enduring and most successful era began with the partnership with Ducati in 2005.

Years with major wins

    The team's major successes have come in recent years, particularly since its partnership with Ducati intensified. The team has been a consistent frontrunner, with numerous race wins and a historic team championship in 2023
