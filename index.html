MOTO GP

WHAT IS MOTO GP?

 

MotoGP is the premier motorcycle racing championship in the world,featuring the fastest bikes and best riders.
    It is organized by the Fédération Internationale de Motocyclisme (FIM) which is also known as Formula 1 of motorcycle racing.

Importance of MotoGP
  • Promotes innovationand technologyin motorcycles.
  • Creates hugeglobal fan following and sports entertrainment.
  • Contributes to the economy , tourism and motorsport culture.
  • Inspires safety and performance improvements in commercial bikes .
